    "hi" in English

    See all translations
    hi exclamation
    uk ​ /ha/ us ​ /ha/ informal

    A1 used as an informal greeting, usually to people who you know:

    Hi, there!
    Hi, how are you doing?

    More examples

    • Love from Sophie. PS Say hi to Gemma.
    • Hi, Lewis. How are you?
    • Oh hi, Ben.
